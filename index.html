Why Try this: Lechon

In the Philippines, they saw the many religions of the country, so they promoted pork in order to spread their culture and religion. Lechon is certainly one of the Philippines' national dishes that is commonly served during feasts, fiestas, holidays, celebrations, and gatherings for family and friends.
